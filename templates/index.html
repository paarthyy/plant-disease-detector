Disease Reference
Know what to look for
Three classes our AI distinguishes with near-perfect accuracy.
🌿

Healthy Plant

No pathogen detected

Uniform green colour, no spots, lesions, or wilting. Leaf surface intact with normal venation. Continue regular monitoring and preventive care.

● No Action Needed
🍂

Early Blight

Alternaria solani

Circular dark brown lesions with concentric rings, surrounded by yellow halo. Starts on older leaves. Fungal — spreads through spores in humid conditions.

⚠ Act Within 3–5 Days
🦠

Late Blight

Phytophthora infestans

Water-soaked lesions that turn brown-black rapidly. White fungal growth on leaf undersides. Spreads explosively in cool, wet weather. Caused the Irish Famine.

🚨 Urgent — 24 Hours
